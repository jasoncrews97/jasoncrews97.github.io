Food Safety and Inspection Website

There is a common saying that goes, "you are what you eat". Every single organism on Earth depends on food to sustain themselves in their day-to-day lives. Since food is such an integral part of everyone's lives, we obviously consider food safety and quality to be issues of great importance. Contaminated food poses a major public health risk, and people tragically die from foodborne illnesses every year. Our website will allow you to discover information about several restaurants in the Prince George's County area and how seriously they take their food safety and overall cleanliness. We hope that you will be able to use our work to make safe and informed decisions next time you are choosing where to go out to eat. Stay healthy!
